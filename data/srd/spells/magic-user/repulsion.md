---
title: Repulsion
type: Arcane Abjuration
level: Magic User 6
range: 10 ft/level
duration: 1 round/2 levels
aoe: 10 ft wide path
components: V, S, M
cast: 6 segments
save: None
draft: false
---

All living creatures in the spell’s path will move away from the caster at their normal movement rates for the spell’s duration, as if by choice.
